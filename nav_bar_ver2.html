About the Founder

Founded by Jane Evergreen, a passionate environmentalist and entrepreneur, Green E-commerce Hub was born out of the belief that shopping shouldn’t cost the Earth. With a background in sustainable agriculture and years of experience in green startups, Jane is committed to making eco-friendly living accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

This platform offers a selection of products that promote sustainable living—ranging from fresh organic vegetables and fruits to eco-friendly household goods. We aim to minimize environmental impact while supporting ethical practices across all our operations. Green E-commerce Hub is not just a store; it’s a lifestyle movement.
